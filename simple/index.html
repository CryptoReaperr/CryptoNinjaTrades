Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our trading signals service.

How many signals do you provide each week?

We typically provide 5-10 trading signals per week, depending on market conditions. We prioritize quality over quantity, so during volatile or uncertain market periods, we may send fewer but higher-confidence signals.

What is the average duration of your trades?

Our trading strategy includes a mix of timeframes. Most trades range from a few hours to a few days, with occasional longer-term positions that may last 1-2 weeks. We clearly indicate the expected timeframe with each signal.

What's your recommended position size and leverage?

We recommend using 2-5% of your total capital per trade, with leverage between 3x-5x for beginners and up to 10x for experienced traders. This conservative approach helps manage risk while still offering good profit potential. Our educational resources cover position sizing in depth.

Do you provide entry, take-profit, and stop-loss levels?

Yes, every signal includes specific entry price/range, multiple take-profit targets, and a clear stop-loss level. We also provide updates if market conditions change, including adjustments to targets or stop-loss positions.

Which exchanges are compatible with your signals?

Our signals work with all major crypto futures exchanges, including Binance, Bybit, OKX, and others. We focus on the most liquid trading pairs which are available on virtually all platforms. If you're unsure about your exchange compatibility, contact us before subscribing.

Can beginners follow your trading signals?

Yes, we design our signals to be accessible to traders of all levels. For beginners, we recommend starting with our educational content first to understand basic futures concepts. Our signals include detailed explanations and we're always available to answer questions in the community chat.
