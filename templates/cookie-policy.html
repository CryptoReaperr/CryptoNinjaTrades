Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They allow websites to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or login status.

How we use cookies

Crypto Ninja Trades uses cookies to enhance your experience on our website, remember your preferences, and provide you with relevant content. We also use cookies to analyze how our website is used and to optimize its performance.

Types of cookies we use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, session management, and user authentication. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the website cannot function properly without them.

  • session - Manages your session information
  • csrf_token - Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • crypto_ninja_cookie_consent - Stores your cookie preferences

Preference Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

  • theme_mode - Remembers your preference for light or dark theme
  • animation_mode - Stores your animation preferences
  • ui_settings - Remembers customized interface settings

Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, including which pages visitors go to most often and if they receive error messages. They help us understand and improve how the website works.

  • crypto_ninja_analytics - Used for anonymous usage statistics
  • _ga - Google Analytics visitor tracking
  • page_views - Tracks which pages you view during your session

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. They are used to display ads that are relevant and engaging for individual users and thereby more valuable for publishers and third-party advertisers.

  • visit_count - Tracks number of visits to tailor marketing
  • referral_source - Records where you came from
  • crypto_interests - Remembers cryptocurrencies you showed interest in

Managing your cookie preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" button below. This will allow you to select which types of cookies you accept and which you don't.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies and to delete them. The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser, and from version to version.

Changes to our cookie policy

We may update our cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified to you when you next visit our website.
